1. CUISINE TYPES & CHARACTERISTICS
===========================================

ITALIAN CUISINE:
- Main Dishes: Pasta (spaghetti, penne, fettuccine, linguine), Pizza (margherita, pepperoni, quattro formaggi), Risotto, Lasagna, Ravioli, Gnocchi
- Appetizers: Bruschetta, Caprese salad, Antipasto, Arancini
- Desserts: Tiramisu, Panna cotta, Gelato, Cannoli
- Key Ingredients: Tomatoes, olive oil, basil, mozzarella, parmesan, garlic
- Characteristics: Rich, savory, cheese-heavy, tomato-based sauces
- Meal Timing: Lunch, Dinner
- Price Range: Mid-range to Premium

CHINESE CUISINE:
- Main Dishes: Fried rice, Chow mein, Lo mein, Sweet & sour chicken/pork, Kung pao chicken, General Tso's chicken, Mapo tofu
- Appetizers: Spring rolls, Dumplings (steamed/fried), Egg rolls, Wontons
- Soups: Hot & sour soup, Wonton soup, Egg drop soup
- Key Ingredients: Soy sauce, ginger, garlic, sesame oil, rice vinegar
- Characteristics: Savory, umami-rich, often stir-fried, balance of flavors
- Meal Timing: Lunch, Dinner
- Price Range: Budget to Mid-range

AMERICAN/FAST FOOD:
- Main Dishes: Burgers, Hot dogs, Fried chicken, BBQ ribs, Mac & cheese, Steaks
- Sides: French fries, Onion rings, Coleslaw, Mashed potatoes
- Sandwiches: Club sandwich, BLT, Philly cheesesteak, Grilled cheese
- Key Characteristics: Hearty portions, fried foods, comfort food
- Meal Timing: Lunch, Dinner, Snacks
- Price Range: Budget to Mid-range

MEXICAN CUISINE:
- Main Dishes: Tacos, Burritos, Quesadillas, Enchiladas, Fajitas, Nachos
- Sides: Refried beans, Mexican rice, Guacamole, Salsa, Pico de gallo
- Key Ingredients: Tortillas, beans, cheese, jalapeños, cilantro, lime
- Characteristics: Spicy, flavorful, cheese and bean-heavy
- Meal Timing: Lunch, Dinner, Snacks
- Price Range: Budget to Mid-range

MIDDLE EASTERN/ARABIC CUISINE:
- Main Dishes: Shawarma, Kebabs, Falafel, Hummus, Grilled meats, Mansaf, Kabsa
- Sides: Tabbouleh, Fattoush, Baba ganoush, Pita bread
- Key Ingredients: Chickpeas, tahini, lamb, chicken, yogurt, spices
- Characteristics: Grilled meats, aromatic spices, healthy options
- Meal Timing: Lunch, Dinner
- Price Range: Budget to Mid-range

INDIAN CUISINE:
- Main Dishes: Curry (chicken tikka masala, butter chicken), Biryani, Tandoori chicken, Samosas, Naan bread
- Vegetarian: Dal, Paneer dishes, Vegetable curry
- Key Ingredients: Curry spices, turmeric, cumin, coriander, garam masala
- Characteristics: Spicy, aromatic, rich sauces, vegetarian-friendly
- Meal Timing: Lunch, Dinner
- Price Range: Budget to Mid-range

JAPANESE CUISINE:
- Main Dishes: Sushi, Sashimi, Ramen, Teriyaki, Tempura, Udon, Donburi
- Appetizers: Edamame, Gyoza, Miso soup
- Key Ingredients: Rice, soy sauce, wasabi, ginger, seaweed
- Characteristics: Fresh, light, balanced flavors, seafood-heavy
- Meal Timing: Lunch, Dinner
- Price Range: Mid-range to Premium

THAI CUISINE:
- Main Dishes: Pad Thai, Green/Red curry, Tom Yum soup, Fried rice, Basil chicken
- Key Ingredients: Lemongrass, coconut milk, fish sauce, Thai basil, chilies
- Characteristics: Balance of sweet, sour, salty, spicy
- Meal Timing: Lunch, Dinner
- Price Range: Budget to Mid-range

MEDITERRANEAN CUISINE:
- Main Dishes: Grilled fish, Greek salad, Moussaka, Souvlaki, Gyros
- Sides: Olives, Feta cheese, Tzatziki, Dolmas
- Key Ingredients: Olive oil, lemon, herbs, feta, tomatoes
- Characteristics: Healthy, fresh, grilled meats, vegetable-rich
- Meal Timing: Lunch, Dinner
- Price Range: Mid-range to Premium

3. DIETARY RESTRICTIONS & PREFERENCES
===========================================

VEGETARIAN:
Can Eat: Vegetables, fruits, grains, dairy, eggs, beans, nuts
Cannot Eat: Meat, poultry, fish, seafood
Recommended Dishes: Vegetable pasta, Veggie pizza, Salads, Falafel, Paneer dishes, Veggie burgers, Grilled vegetables

VEGAN:
Can Eat: Vegetables, fruits, grains, beans, nuts, seeds
Cannot Eat: All animal products (meat, dairy, eggs, honey)
Recommended Dishes: Vegan pasta (no cheese), Vegetable stir-fry, Hummus, Falafel, Fruit smoothies, Vegan burgers, Tofu dishes

HALAL:
Can Eat: Halal-certified meat, chicken, fish, vegetables, grains
Cannot Eat: Pork, alcohol, non-halal meat
Recommended Dishes: Halal chicken, Halal beef, Shawarma, Kebabs, Grilled meats, Fish dishes

GLUTEN-FREE:
Can Eat: Rice, quinoa, corn, potatoes, meat, fish, vegetables, fruits
Cannot Eat: Wheat, barley, rye (bread, pasta, pizza crust)
Recommended Dishes: Rice bowls, Grilled meats, Salads, Gluten-free pasta, Rice noodles

KETO/LOW-CARB:
Focus: High fat, moderate protein, very low carbs
Can Eat: Meat, fish, eggs, cheese, low-carb vegetables, nuts
Avoid: Bread, pasta, rice, potatoes, sugar, most fruits
Recommended Dishes: Grilled meats, Salads (no croutons), Eggs, Cheese-based dishes, Seafood

HIGH-PROTEIN:
Focus: 20g+ protein per meal
Recommended: Grilled chicken, Steak, Fish, Eggs, Protein bowls, Greek yogurt, Tofu
Good for: Post-workout, muscle building, weight management

LOW-CALORIE:
Focus: Under 500 calories per meal
Recommended: Salads, Grilled fish, Steamed vegetables, Soup, Light wraps
Good for: Weight loss, healthy eating

10. RECOMMENDATION LOGIC & RULES
===========================================

WHEN USER SAYS "LUNCH":
1. Check time of day (11 AM - 3 PM ideal)
2. Recommend MAIN COURSES (pasta, burgers, rice bowls, salads)
3. DO NOT recommend just coffee, snacks, or drinks
4. Portion size: Moderate (not too heavy)
5. Prep time: Ideally under 30 minutes

WHEN USER SAYS "DINNER":
1. Check time of day (5 PM - 10 PM ideal)
2. Recommend FULL MEALS (steaks, seafood, pasta, curry)
3. Portion size: Larger, more filling
4. Can be heavier and richer
5. Prep time: Can be longer (30-45 min acceptable)

WHEN USER SAYS "ITALIAN FOOD":
1. ONLY recommend Italian dishes (pasta, pizza, risotto)
2. DO NOT recommend Chinese, Mexican, or other cuisines
3. Mention Italian restaurants specifically
4. Include Italian characteristics (tomato-based, cheese, etc.)

WHEN USER SAYS "HEALTHY":
1. Focus on grilled, not fried
2. Include calorie information if available
3. Mention protein content
4. Recommend salads, grilled meats, vegetables
5. Avoid heavy sauces, fried foods

WHEN USER SAYS "QUICK" or "FAST":
1. Prep time under 15 minutes
2. Recommend fast food, sandwiches, wraps
3. Mention quick service restaurants
4. Avoid fine dining or slow-cooked items

WHEN USER SAYS "SPICY":
1. Check spice_level field (3-5)
2. Recommend curry, wings, Thai, Mexican
3. Mention spice level explicitly
4. Offer mild alternatives if needed
